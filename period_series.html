My Period Series

A quarterly magazine designed specifically for teenage girls.

Home

Menstruation, often called a period, is a natural and essential part of every girl's journey into womanhood. It’s a sign that your body is growing and functioning as it should. Yet, for many, it comes with a mix of emotions, questions, and even challenges. Unfortunately, stigma and misinformation have made periods seem like something to be ashamed of—but they are nothing to hide. Every girl deserves access to accurate information, proper hygiene, and a supportive environment to manage her period with confidence and dignity. Whether it’s your first period or you’ve had it for years, understanding your cycle helps you take control of your health and well-being. This space is dedicated to breaking the silence, providing guidance, and ensuring that every girl feels empowered on her menstrual journey.

As part of our comprehensive mentorship program, we are proud to introduce "My Period Series," a quarterly magazine designed specifically for teenage girls. This engaging publication serves as a trusted resource, addressing the unique concerns, interests, and experiences of our target audience.



Through "My Period Series," we aim to:

  • Break down stigmas and taboos surrounding menstruation

  • Promote healthy habits and menstruation awareness

  • Encourage open communication, empathy, and understanding among girls and their support networks

  • Educate young girls on menstruation and nurture their confidence during puberty



Content Features

  • Stories from different girls and women describing their first period experience.

  • Creative expressions, such as artwork, poetry, and short stories

  • The Doctor’s/Nurse’s Corner: Doctor/Nurse answers some of the frequently asked questions about menstruation

  • Menstrual health informative articles and menstrual hygiene tips

  • Myths vs Truths about periods

  • Quiz questions, games, and crossword puzzles for you to fill in and learn!
